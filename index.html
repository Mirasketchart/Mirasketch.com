About Me

Taylor creates character driven illustrations inspired by fairy tales and feminine fantasy. She seamlessly blends 2d characters and 3d modelled environments. She’s best known for her webcomic Arcturus Rising which blends rich fantasy worlds with the magical girl genre.

Based in Vancouver BC, this artist is proficient in 2d illustration and comic work as well as 3D environmental + character creation in both Blender and Maya.
